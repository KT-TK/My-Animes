This site contains the list of all the animes and mangas that i have watched or read, as well as the sites where you can read and watch them.

Here are all the animes that I have watched.

Attack On Titan

Giant Titans with humanoid form are trying to destroy all humanity when Eren Yeager makes a promise to himself to put an end to all Titans and save the humans from extinction.

Death Note

When a Japenese high schooler comes into possesion of a mystical notebook, he finds he has power to kill anybody whose name he enters in it. Using his new-found powers he decides to kill all the criminals in the world.

No Game No Life

Legendary gamer siblings Sora and Shiro are transported to a world where life is a series of games and humanity is in grave danger of extinction.

Re:Zero

On his way home from the convenience store, teenager Subaru Natsuki is transported to another world, where he gains the power to reverse time by dying.

Demon Slayer

After a demon attack leaves his family slain and his sister cursed, Tanjiro embarks upon a perilous journey to find a cure and avenge those he's lost.

Steins;Gate

Rintaro Okabe, a self-proclaimed mad scientist,along with his freinds develop a machine that can send messages through phones, back in time.

Darling in the Franxx

In a future world where humanity has been driven to endangerment by giant beasts, a strike force is assembled to destroy the monsters and save the world.

Kabaneri of the Iron Fortress

Ikoma and the Iron Fortress take their fight to the battlegrounds of Unato, joining the alliance to reclaim the region fro the kabane horde.

Jujutsu Kaisen

A boy swallows a cursed talisman - the finger of a demon - and becomes cursed himself. He enters a shaman's school to be able to locate the demon's other body parts and thus exorcise himself.

Mahou Shoujo Site

Aya Asagiri, is a middle school girl who has problems both at school with bullying and at home from physical abuse by her brother. While browsing online, a website pops up on her computer featuring a creepy looking person. This person appears to take pity on her, and announces that she has granted Asagiri magical powers.
